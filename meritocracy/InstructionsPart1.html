In this task, you can work on as many puzzles as you like — the total number of puzzles exceeds what most participants can complete.

To determine your bonus earnings in this part, you will be paired with another participant in this study. Your bonus depends on how your performance compares to theirs.

  • If you correctly solve a larger number of puzzles than your paired participant, you will win and receive a bonus of {{ session.config.prize }}.
  • If there is a tie (that is, each of you solves the same number of puzzles), each of you is equally likely to win.

You will now start the task. In each puzzle, you have a time limit of 60 seconds.

Note that you must spend at least 40 seconds on a puzzle before moving to the next one.

You may stop solving puzzles at any time and continue with Part 2.
