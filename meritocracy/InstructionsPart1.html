This study has two parts. At the end of the study, one part will be randomly selected to determine your bonus earnings.

In Part 1, you will be asked to solve a series of puzzles. Each puzzle consists of eight images, with one image missing. You will identify the missing image that completes the pattern in each puzzle.

To determine your bonus earnings in this part, you will be paired with another participant of the study. Your earnings depend on your performance relative to that of your paired participant as follows:

  • If you correctly solve a larger number of puzzles than your paired participant, you will win and receive a bonus of {{ session.config.prize }}.
  • If there is a tie (that is, each of you solves the same number of puzzles), each of you is equally likely to win.

You will now start the task. In each puzzle, you will have a time limit of 60 seconds.

You can stop the task at any time and continue with Part 2.
